How We Build Reliable Data

From raw headlines and images to clean, labeled datasets.

Data Collection

News articles, social-media posts, and fact-checked reports are collected from trusted sources and verified misinformation databases.

Cleaning & Pre-processing

Text is normalized, tokenized, and stripped of noise. Images are resized, aligned, and filtered to highlight key facial features for deepfake analysis.

Labeling & Balancing

Each sample is labeled as REAL or FAKE. Datasets are balanced to avoid bias and split into train, validation, and test sets.

Under the Hood

The complete AI pipeline from input to prediction.

01

Unified Input

The system accepts both text and image in a single interface. You can paste a headline, upload an image, or do both together.

02

Dual-Model Engine

BERT analyzes linguistic cues, while XceptionNet inspects pixel-level artifacts to detect visual manipulation.

03

Fusion & Verdict

Results are combined into a clear verdict with a confidence score, so users can quickly decide whether to trust the content.
